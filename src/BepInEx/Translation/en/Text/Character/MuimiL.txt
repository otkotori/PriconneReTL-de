##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、相棒と共に明日へと進む大悪党の少女。自己強化や物防ダウンを駆使するムイミと、ＵＢ後に一定時間共闘するオクトーの追撃で、輝く未来を取り戻す。=[Physical] A villainous girl who advances with her partner in vanguard. With the pursuit of Muimi, who makes full use of self-buffing and P.DEF down, and Octo, who fights together for a certain period of time after UB, she regains her bright future.
