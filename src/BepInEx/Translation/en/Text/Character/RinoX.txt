##Profile##
【物理】後衛で、攻撃と支援を担う聖夜のアーチャー。物理攻撃力と物理クリティカルアップスキルで周囲を強化し、クリティカルで威力が増大するＵＢで敵を粉砕する。=[Physical] A Christmas archer who is in charge of offense and support in the rearguard. She uses her physical attack and physical critical boost skills to strengthen her allies, then crushes enemies with her critical and powerful UB.
