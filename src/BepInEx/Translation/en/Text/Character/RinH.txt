##Profile##
【物理】中衛で敵を悪戯で翻弄する恐怖のキョンシー娘。最も魔法攻撃力が高い敵に急襲を仕掛け、ＴＰ減少や魔法攻撃力ダウン、スタン等の妨害スキルで機先を制する。=[Physical] A terrifying Jiangxi girl who plays tricks on her enemies in midlines. She launches a surprise attack on the enemy with the highest M.ATK, and uses obstructive skills such as TP reduction, M.ATK reduction, and stun to get ahead.
