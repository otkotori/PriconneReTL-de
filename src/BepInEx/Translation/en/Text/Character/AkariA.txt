##Profile##
【魔法】中衛で、魔法攻撃をお見舞いする双子の妹天使。効果時間が短い分、絶大な効果の強化魔法で自身の攻撃力を大きく高め、威力抜群の単体魔法で敵を一掃する。=[Magic] A twin sister angel who deals magical damage in the midlines. Since her effect time is short, her powerful strengthening magic greatly increases her own attack power, and she wipes out enemies with her extremely powerful single magic.
