##Profile##
【魔法】後衛で後ろの味方を強化する魔法少女の看板娘。後衛の味方をバリアとＴＰ継続回復で支援し、自分の魔法攻撃力に応じ強化される攻撃力アップスキルで鼓舞する。=[Magic] A magical girl who strengthens the backline allies. She supports her backline allies with barriers and TP recovery, and encourages them with her attack boost skill that is strengthened according to her own magic attack power.
