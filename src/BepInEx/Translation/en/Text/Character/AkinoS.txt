##Profile##
【物理】前衛で、優雅なバカンスを過ごす夕映えの令嬢。敵が魔法攻撃をする場合に効果を大幅に上げる２種の範囲攻撃と攻防両面の支援スキルで、盛夏の勝鬨を上げる。=[Physical] A young heiress basking in the sunset, spending her vacation on the frontlines with grace. She rouses her summer-time spirit with both offensive and defensive support, along with her two AoE attacks that all increase with effectiveness when facing an enemy wielding magic.
